# Quick Reference - GuideOn Meeting

## Elevator Pitch (30 seconds)

"Jesse, I built something I think you'll find interesting. This is a working demo of what GuideOn's virtual assistant could look like. It handles routine questions 24/7, qualifies leads, and routes hot prospects to your sales team. The bot opens, humans close - exactly what you mentioned about AI + human contact."

## Demo Flow

1. **Show the chatbot** - Open demo URL
2. **Try quote request:** "I need a freight quote"
   - Watch it collect origin, destination, cargo details
   - See how it captures contact info
3. **Try stats question:** "What's your on-time delivery rate?"
   - Shows it knows GuideOn's stats (99%)
4. **Try carrier question:** "How do I become an owner-operator?"
   - Demonstrates carrier recruitment use case

## Key Talking Points

- **24/7 Availability** - Never miss a quote request
- **Lead Qualification** - Collects all info before routing to sales
- **Cost Efficiency** - Handles routine questions for pennies
- **Low-Risk Pilot** - 30-day trial on one use case

## Pricing Ranges

- **Quick win:** $2,000-3,000 for chatbot integration
- **Full solution:** $5,000-8,000 for chatbot + website refresh
- **Ongoing:** $500-1,000/month for maintenance + improvements

## Closing Lines

**If interested:**
"Great! I can have this customized and live on your site within 2 weeks. What's the best way to move forward?"

**If hesitant:**
"No pressure at all. This is just one example of what we can do. What other challenges are you facing that we might be able to help with?"

## Questions to Ask

1. "How do you currently handle quote requests that come in after hours?"
2. "What percentage of your leads require immediate response vs. can wait?"
3. "How much time does your team spend answering routine questions?"
4. "What would it be worth to never miss a lead again?"
